We are seeking an accomplished people leader and a highly skilled analyst to build Operations Data Analytics at 1Password. As the Manager of Operations Data Analytics, you will have the opportunity to build and guide a team of analysts who will collaborate closely with internal teams (e.g., RevOps, Sales, Engineering, etc.) to build and maintain executive-level dashboards and conduct analysis on factors relating to conversion, customer satisfaction, deployment, and more. Your analysis will influence and inform our go-to-market, customer support, product strategy, processes, and execution.

This is a remote opportunity within Canada and the US.
What we're looking for:
1-3 years managing people and 5+ years in a Data Analytics role or related with a preference for experience in a technology/SaaS environment.  
Strong leadership skills combined with a hands-on approach and ability to guide a team through ambiguity: We are looking for someone who can effectively balance their time between actively participating in analytics projects, proactively identifying and addressing data issues, and nurturing the growth of their team's analytical and leadership capabilities.
Compelling communication skills: You are a skilled communicator with a demonstrated ability to influence decisions through communicating technical concepts and complex data and insights to a variety of stakeholders in diverse settings. 
Versatile analytics skills: Demonstrated experience in various analytics areas domains with a proven ability to identify customer groups, calculate return on investment (ROI), conduct A/B testing, create insightful dashboards, and analyze customer satisfaction. 
Data infrastructure and modeling knowledge: A solid understanding of data infrastructure and data modeling principles is essential to ensure the accuracy and reliability of analytics processes.
Proficiency in relevant tools and technologies: Expert-level skills in SQL, intermediate proficiency in Python (including libraries like NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib), familiarity with Python scripting for automation and data ingestion, and extensive experience with business intelligence tools (particularly Looker, but experience with Tableau, PowerBI, and others is welcome) are expected.
Structured and guiding approach: You have experience guiding a team through the entire analytics lifecycle, defining success metrics, crafting analytics plans, and seeing projects through to completion.

A dynamic management position offering tremendous growth opportunities, allowing you to succeed as a leader and shape the future of product development at 1Password.
Lead the recruitment efforts and establish a robust operations data analytics team, finding the perfect balance between projects and priorities as the team expands. If you can envision it, we can make it a reality.

Manage the Operations Data Analytics staff, providing direction, support, and resources they need to perform and grow. Responsible for the development, engagement, and performance of the team.
